    <article class="entry entry--featured">
      <div class="entry__marker">Recent</div>
      <div class="entry__body">
        <h3 class="entry__title">Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi</h3>
        <p class="entry__subtitle">Molecular Dynamics Simulation — Research Internship</p>
        <ul class="entry__list">
          <li>Ran classical molecular dynamics (MD) simulations in <strong>GROMACS</strong> to study how atomic-scale structure governs the stability and dynamic behaviour of the system under investigation, sampling trajectories across multiple simulated timescales to capture equilibrium and near-equilibrium behaviour.</li>
          <li>Set up simulation systems end-to-end — topology generation, solvation/energy minimization, NVT/NPT equilibration, and production runs — within a Linux (Ubuntu) environment.</li>
          <li>Analyzed trajectories for structural and energetic descriptors (e.g. RMSD/RMSF, radial distribution functions, interaction energies) using <strong>VMD</strong> and Python (NumPy, Matplotlib) to connect simulation output back to physical/chemical interpretation.</li>
          <li>Strengthened working understanding of force-field–based modeling and how MD complements experimental and quantum-chemical approaches to studying molecular systems.</li>
        </ul>
